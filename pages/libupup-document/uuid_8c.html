int uuid_compare ( uuid_upnp * u1,
uuid_upnp * u2 )

Compare two UUID's "lexically" and return.

Compare two UUID's "lexically".

  • -1: u1 is lexically before u2
  • 0: u1 is equal to u2
  • 1: u1 is lexically after u2

Note: Lexical ordering is not temporal ordering!
